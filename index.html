Honors

Wolf-Hallac Award (2020)
The Wolf-Hallac Award was established in October 2000 to be awarded to the best graduating female senior from Penn Engineering who is seen as a role model, has achieved a high GPA (top 10%) of class and who has demonstrated a commitment to school and or community.

Outstanding Service Award (2020)
For significant contributions that enhance the social well-being of other Penn Engineering students. Selected winners typically hold leadership roles in student societies within Penn Engineering, have served on social committees within individual masters programs, or have carried out extraordinary acts that benefit other students.

J.P. Eckert Fellowship (2019)
The J.P. Eckert Fellowship honors J. Presper Eckert, co-inventor of the first general-purpose electronic digital computer, ENIAC, at the University of Pennsylvania. This Fellowship will provide six outstanding master’s students who are U.S. citizens or U.S. permanent residents $10,000 towards tuition. The purpose of the Fellowship is to provide financial support to masters students and to support the University’s interest in obtaining educational benefits by promoting student body diversity.

Victor W. K. Ku Memorial Award (2019)
The Victor W. Ku Memorial Award is presented annually to one student in the Department of Systems, and one in the Department of Mechanical Engineering and Applied Mechanics, who at the end of their junior year best exemplify the ideals of high scholarship, personal discipline, and service to others for which Victor W.
